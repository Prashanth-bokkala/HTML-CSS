Some of my favorite Movies

JERSEY

In "Jersey," the father-son dynamic forms the emotional core of the narrative. Nani's character,Arjun, undergoes a poignant journey, driven by the profound love for his son. The film beautifully depicts the sacrifices and unwavering support a father provides to fulfill his child's dreams. The emotional resonance of their relationship becomes a compelling force, propelling Arjun to overcome obstacles and pursue an improbable cricket comeback. Simultaneously,the love story between Nani and the heroine adds a tender layer to the narrative. Their relationship unfolds with authenticity, showcasing moments of joy, vulnerability, and shared aspirations. "Jersey" masterfully weaves these emotional threads, creating a film that goes beyond sports, delving into the intricacies of human connections and the enduring power of love.

AKASHAM NEE HADHURA

Its a movie one inspired me that a life partner is also a crucial part of success.Inspired by the book 'Simply Fly', the film tells the story of Nedumaaran Rajangam known to friends as Maara, the son of a teacher, who sets out to make common man fly and takes on the most capital intensive industry in the world with the help of his friends, family and sheer will power.

M.S.Dhoni

The movie takes the audience on a journey through Dhoni's life, starting from his early days in Ranchi to his rise in the world of cricket. It provides insights into his personal and professional challenges, showcasing his determination, leadership qualities, and the impact of his unconventional captaincy style on Indian cricket. Sushant Singh Rajput's portrayal of Dhoni received widespread acclaim for its authenticity and depth, capturing the nuances of the cricketer's personality. The film successfully blends drama, emotion, and cricketing action, making it a compelling watch for both cricket enthusiasts and those interested in human stories of perseverance and success

Chhichhore

The film's title, "Chhichhore," translates to 'mischievous' or 'carefree,' reflecting the carefree spirit of the characters during their college days. Sushant Singh Rajput and Shraddha Kapoor play lead roles in this coming-of-age comedy-drama. "Chhichhore" received positive reviews for its engaging storytelling, relatable characters, and the valuable life lessons it imparts. It delivers a strong message about dealing with failure and celebrating the spirit of friendship. The film strikes a balance between humor and emotional depth, making it an enjoyable and heartwarming experience for audiences.

3 idiotsA

The film dexplores themes of education, societal expectations, and the pursuit of passion. Rancho, the central character, challenges traditional norms of education and encourages his friends to follow their true passions rather than conforming to societal expectations. The narrative alternates between the characters' college days and the present as they embark on a journey to find their missing friend Rancho. "3 Idiots" is celebrated for its humor, impactful storytelling, and thought-provoking messages about the flaws in the education system. It became one of the highest-grossing Bollywood films and resonated with audiences for its memorable characters and the powerful performances of the cast. The film encourages viewers to question conventional notions of success and happiness.
